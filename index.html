ABOUT

GibSpree is an FPS streamer who doesn't just frag enemies. He leaves nothing behind. Born from the chaos of arena shooters, raised on killstreaks and carnage. Every session is a spree. Every spree ends in gibs.

gib /ɡɪb/ - noun, verb, way of life.

Originating from Quake and Doom (circa 1996), a gib is what happens when you overkill an enemy so hard their body explodes into bloody chunks. Short for giblets. The rocket doesn't just kill. It deletes.

A gib spree? That's when you're on such a tear that nothing is left standing. Or intact.

Now you know. You're welcome.
